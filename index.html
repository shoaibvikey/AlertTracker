How to Use Alert Tracker

⚠️ Important Note About Your Data Currently, this Alert Tracker is designed for active, single-session tracking. Refreshing your web browser or closing the tab will clear your current alert history. Keep the tab open while you are actively working your shift!

1. Understanding the Dashboard

  • The Header (Top): Provides a quick view of your overall metrics: Total Alerts, Active Alerts (Assigned + Escalated), and Closed Alerts.
  • The Input & Summary Section (Left): Your control center. Log new alerts and see breakdowns of your data by Status, Priority, and Queue.
  • The Alerts Log (Right): A scrolling historical table of every alert logged during your current session.

2. How to Add a New Alert

  1. Select the Queue the alert belongs to from the dropdown.
  2. Select the Priority level (P0 - P3).
  3. Select the current Status.
  4. Click the blue Add Alert button. Your metrics and logs will update instantly.

3. How to Edit an Existing Alert

  1. Find the alert in the Recent Alerts Log on the right.
  2. Click the yellow Edit button. The input form will populate with the alert's details.
  3. Make your changes and click Update Alert.

4. How to Delete an Alert

  • Find the alert in the log and click the red Del button.
  • Confirm the deletion in the popup. All counters will automatically recalculate.
